📖 Game Description / 游戏简介

Stray in Score is a 3D-to-2D level-transitioning audio-reactive platform game. During an ordinary night when a music composer is working, you descend into his dreams, collecting scattered notes to seek inspiration within his subconscious.

The game blends precision platforming with music games — players must listen to the soundtrack to anticipate the movement of spike traps and shifting platforms. A mistimed jump will send you plummeting into the abyss or impaled on deadly thorns.

As the levels progress, the music grows more frenetic, the terrain becomes increasingly surreal, and even new, unpredictable hazards emerge...

Can you gather all the lost fragments of inspiration, help the composer complete his masterpiece, and make him self-reconciliation?

《Stray in Score》是一款 3D转2D视角切换的音乐反应平台跳跃游戏。在一个普通的夜晚，作曲家正在工作中沉沉睡去，你坠入他的梦境，收集散落的音符，在他的潜意识中寻找灵感。

游戏结合了精准平台跳跃与音乐节奏玩法——玩家必须聆听背景音乐，预判尖刺陷阱和移动平台的时机。一个失误的跳跃，便会让你坠入深渊或被尖刺刺穿。

随着关卡推进，音乐变得愈发狂乱，地形也更加 surreal（超现实），甚至会出现全新的、不可预测的障碍……

你能收集所有失落的灵感碎片，帮助作曲家完成他的杰作，并让他与自我和解吗？

🗺️ Level Guide / 关卡指南
Level 1 — Tutorial / 第一关：教学关卡

Level 1 is a tutorial stage with low difficulty and a modest BPM, designed to teach players the basic controls.

第一关为教学关卡，难度较低，BPM 适中，旨在让玩家熟悉基本操作。

Level 2 — Route Planning / 第二关：路线规划

Level 2 centers on route planning. Notes are scattered across the stage and can be collected via multiple paths, so players must carefully plan their route.

第二关的核心是路线规划。音符散落在关卡各处，可通过多条路径收集，玩家需要仔细规划行进路线。

Level 3 — The Final Challenge / 第三关：最终挑战

Level 3 is the most challenging stage, combining platforming with puzzle-solving. It also features the fastest BPM. Numerous obstacles — such as cannon fire and moving blocks — must be cleared before players can proceed.

第三关是最具挑战性的关卡，结合了平台跳跃与解谜元素，同时拥有最快的 BPM。玩家需要清除大量障碍物——如炮火和移动平台——才能继续前进。
